# 🔬 Quantum Sensing

## Overview

This repository contains my study notes, technical documentation and learning material related to quantum sensing with nitrogen-vacancy (NV) centers in diamond.

The content is based on my summer research internship at the Nanomaterials and Nanotechnology Research Center (CINN), where I am learning the fundamentals of quantum sensing, microwave instrumentation and experimental techniques.

- Optical pumping

- Radio-frequency instrumentation
- Spectrum analysis
